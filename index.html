What is ExpenseFlow?

ExpenseFlow is a comprehensive personal finance tool designed for students and professionals to track spending habits in real-time.

What does it do?

It allows you to set monthly limits, record income and expenses, and visualizes your financial health through interactive charts.

Why It Matters

Why Financial Tracking Matters

Studies show that individuals who track their expenses save up to 20% more than those who don't. By identifying hidden costs, you gain the freedom to invest in what truly matters.

📊 Visual Insights See exactly where your money goes with beautiful, interactive charts.
🔔 Budget Discipline Get alerts the moment you're about to exceed your set limits.
🔒 Data Privacy All your data is stored locally on your device — no cloud, no compromise.
