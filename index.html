What is ExpenseFlow?

ExpenseFlow is a comprehensive personal finance tool designed for students and professionals to track spending habits in real-time[cite: 1].

What does it do?

It allows you to set monthly limits, record income/expenses, and visualizes your financial health through interactive charts.

Why Financial Tracking Matters?

Studies show that individuals who track their expenses save up to 20% more than those who don't. By identifying "hidden" costs, you gain the freedom to invest in what truly matters.
