Frequently Asked Questions

What is ExpenseFlow?
ExpenseFlow is a personal finance management platform that helps users manage budgets, bills, savings goals, and transactions.
Does ExpenseFlow support bill reminders?
Yes. Users can add recurring bills, receive due reminders, and track payment status easily.
Can I track my savings goals?
Yes. ExpenseFlow includes a Goal Tracker feature where users can monitor savings progress visually.
What happens when I mark a bill as paid?
Paid bills are automatically moved into the transaction section so users can maintain complete expense history records.
Can I filter my transactions?
Yes. Users can filter transactions based on categories, payment status, and dates for easier tracking.
Does ExpenseFlow generate reports?
Yes. Users can view monthly reports and dashboards to analyze spending, savings, and budgeting patterns.
